## Description
RootSpeak is the flashcard app built specifically for preserving and learning indigenous and endangered languages. Whether you are a heritage speaker reconnecting with your ancestral tongue, a community member documenting elders' wisdom, or a learner passionate about cultural preservation, RootSpeak gives you the tools to create, study, and share language flashcards with respect and purpose.

Unlike generic flashcard apps, RootSpeak puts pronunciation at the center. Record native speaker audio directly on each card so every word carries the voice of your community. Our FSRS-6 spaced repetition algorithm ensures you review at the optimal time, maximizing retention while minimizing study time.

CREATE CUSTOM FLASHCARD DECKS
Build decks for any indigenous language. Add words, phrases, sentences, and cultural notes. Attach images for visual context. Record pronunciation with one tap. Your language, your way.

FSRS-6 SPACED REPETITION
RootSpeak uses the latest FSRS-6 algorithm to schedule your reviews. Rate each card after studying and the algorithm adapts to your memory. Hard cards appear more often. Easy cards fade into long-term intervals. Study smarter, not longer.

AUDIO RECORDING AND PLAYBACK
Every card can carry a native speaker's voice. Record pronunciation directly, play it back during study sessions, and preserve the sounds that make each language unique. Audio is stored privately on your device.

STUDY STATISTICS
Track your daily streak, monitor retention rates, and see your progress over time. Stay motivated with clear, visual feedback on your learning journey.

ICLOUD SYNC
Enable CloudKit sync to access your decks across all your Apple devices. Your data stays in your private iCloud container. We never access or share your content.

COMMUNITY SHARING
Share your decks with other learners and discover decks created by the RootSpeak community. Together, we can keep languages alive.

RootSpeak is designed with cultural sensitivity at its core. We believe language preservation is a community effort, and our app is built to support that mission.
