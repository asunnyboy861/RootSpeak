Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2026

1. Overview

RootSpeak ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

RootSpeak is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect minimal data:

  • Flashcard Data: Decks, cards, audio recordings, and study progress you create are stored locally on your device.
  • CloudKit Data: If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud container. We cannot access this data.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.
  • Personal Data: We do not require account creation, email, or any personal information to use the app.

3. Audio Recordings

Audio recordings you create for pronunciation are stored locally on your device. If CloudKit sync is enabled, recordings are synced through your private iCloud container. We never access, transmit, or store your audio recordings on our servers.

4. Photos and Images

Any photos or images you add to flashcards are stored locally on your device. We use the PhotosUI framework with minimal permissions and do not access your photo library beyond what you explicitly select.

5. Third-Party Services

RootSpeak uses the following Apple services:

  • CloudKit: For optional data synchronization across your Apple devices. Data is encrypted and stored in your private container.
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your flashcard data remains under your control at all times.

7. Data Retention

All data is stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. CloudKit data persists in your iCloud account according to Apple's data retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

RootSpeak is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual decks or cards within the app
  • Disable CloudKit sync at any time in Settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: rootspeak.support@example.com
