Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new flashcard deck?
A: Tap the "+" button on the Decks tab or use "New Deck" from the Home screen. Enter the deck name, select the language, and start adding cards.
Q: How does spaced repetition work?
A: RootSpeak uses the FSRS-6 algorithm. After each card review, you rate how well you knew the answer (Again, Hard, Good, Easy). The algorithm schedules the next review at the optimal time to maximize retention.
Q: How do I record pronunciation?
A: When creating or editing a card, tap the microphone icon to record audio. Tap again to stop. You can preview the recording before saving.
Q: How do I manage my subscription?
A: Go to Settings > Your Apple ID > Subscriptions, or use the "Restore Purchases" option in RootSpeak Settings. You can cancel anytime.
Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable CloudKit sync, data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud container. We never access your data.

Data Recovery

If you need to recover your data:

  1. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID
  2. Enable CloudKit sync in RootSpeak Settings
  3. Wait for sync to complete (may take a few minutes)
  4. If issues persist, contact support
