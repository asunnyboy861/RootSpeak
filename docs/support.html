Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new flashcard deck?

Tap the "+" button on the Decks tab or use "New Deck" from the Home screen. Enter the deck name, select the language, and start adding cards.

How does spaced repetition work?

RootSpeak uses the FSRS-6 algorithm. After each card review, you rate how well you knew the answer (Again, Hard, Good, Easy). The algorithm schedules the next review at the optimal time to maximize retention.

How do I record pronunciation?

When creating or editing a card, tap the microphone icon to record audio. Tap again to stop. You can preview the recording before saving.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable CloudKit sync, data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud container. We never access your data.

Subscription FAQ

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find RootSpeak and tap it
  5. Choose "Cancel Subscription" or change your plan

Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open RootSpeak, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases".

What's the difference between Monthly and Yearly?

Both plans offer the same features. The Yearly plan offers a 44% discount compared to paying monthly.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can continue using premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll revert to the free version with 3 decks and 50 cards per deck.

I purchased Lifetime but it's not showing

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your Lifetime access.

Data Recovery

If you need to recover your data:

  1. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID
  2. Enable CloudKit sync in RootSpeak Settings
  3. Wait for sync to complete (may take a few minutes)
  4. If issues persist, contact support
